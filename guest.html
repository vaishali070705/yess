GUEST POST SUBMISSIONS


Got an idea for an article that would add value to She Owns It? We’d love to hear about it! We don’t reply to every submission but do our best to let you know if your submission has been approved.

Before you write and submit your post, please read the following:

  • Submission of your guest post does not guarantee publication. Your submission will be reviewed and if it will benefit SheOwns It’s readers, it is likely to be published.
  • Links back to your own blog or site should be modest and fit the content of your post. If you include too many links, chances are low it will be published. Make sure any links in your post are relevant and valid. Links deemed to be irrelevant to the main idea of your post will be removed without notice. Likewise, any non-working links will be removed without notice./li>
  • Word count: 00 – 500 words is preferred. The maximum word count is 900. (If you aren’t sure what your word count is, the Word Count Tool is handy.
  • Preference will be given to those who demonstrate a genuine interest in SheOwnsIt.com as a reader or follower. Submissions with a “visit my site” or “check out my product” type feel will not be accepted. Likewise, submissions which appear to be strictly PR- or SEO-related will not be accepted. If your post fits into the sponsored content category you’ll need to pay for a sponsored post and meet our sponsored post guidelines.
  • Posts must be high-quality, original content (i.e. not published anywhere else online).
